Chicken Tikka Vanilla Cake Chicken Tandoor Fish Curry Choclate Cake

  • Recipe of Chicken Tikka

  • INGRDIENTS

  • Tuna - 7 can
  • Potato - 2 (Medium Size)
  • Onion Fried
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Dhone Pata
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Shami Kabab Masala (or Jeera gura + gorom masala gura)
  • Lemon
  • Kacha morich (Just for Flavour)
  • Egg
  • Oil

  • RECIPE

  • Without egg mix everything and make small balls.
  • After that dip the balls into egg and fry it into oil.
